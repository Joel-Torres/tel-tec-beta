Privacy Policy

The purpose of this statement is to define Tel-Tec's policies with regards to the collection and use of personally identifiable information.

We collect information volunteered by the consumer, such as survey information and/or site registrations, email, name and address, telephone number, fax number, payment information (e.g., credit card number and billing address). Tel-Tec is committed to data security and the data quality of personally identifiable information that is either available from or collected by our web site and has taken reasonable precautions to protect such information from loss, misuse or alteration.
